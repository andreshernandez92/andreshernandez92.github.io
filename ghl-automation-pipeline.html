⚙️ How the Automation Works

When an agency spends thousands on ads, a 30-minute delay in contacting a lead can ruin a sale. This advanced multi-step automation acts as a virtual sales assistant.

1

Capture & Tag

Lead submits form and is automatically tagged for CRM segmentation.

2

Initial Nurture

Welcome Email is sent with a calendar link, moving the lead to "Awaiting Reply".

3

Wait Step

The automation pauses for exactly 2 days to give the lead time to act.

4

If/Else Logic

System checks CRM tags to see if an appointment was successfully booked.

5

Smart Follow-Up

If no appointment was booked, an automated follow-up email is sent to re-engage.

6

Close

If booked, pipeline updates to "Appointment Booked" and eventually "Closed Won".

📋 Client Prerequisites for Automation

To implement this advanced Speed-to-Lead architecture for your business, the following basic setup is required:

  • GoHighLevel Account: You must have an active GoHighLevel account (Agency or Sub-Account level).
  • Email Integration: An active SMTP integration (like Mailgun or LeadConnector Email) configured in your GHL settings to ensure welcome emails are delivered reliably.
  • Calendar Setup: A configured GHL Calendar with your availability synced so leads can successfully book a time via the automated link.
  • Dedicated Domain (Optional but Recommended): A registered domain connected to your GHL account to host the lead capture form/landing page professionally.
